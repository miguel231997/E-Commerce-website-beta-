Migs is the perfect place to seek new styles and fashion. Everything is carefully crafted by the owner Miguel who envisions that all styles can come together and create the balanace of stylish yet casual and comfortable apparel. It all began in the comfort of his own home where Miguel would work day and night crafting new clothing that he would give out for free so people could see how unique and comfortobale his style was. Soon he opened up his very own little store right in his garage.Success kept coming and soon he opened up his very first store in Graniteville. Now you can come to our many locations around Staten Island to see for yourself.
